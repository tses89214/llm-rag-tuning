## Stark 4
- **Description**: A family-friendly spaceship with ample space and comfort for long voyages.  
- **Size**: 35 meters in length.  
- **Weight**: 50 metric tons.  
- **Capacity**: 12 passengers.  
- **Color**: Ocean Blue.  
- **Price**: $8,000,000.  
- **Compatible Accessories**: Entertainment System, Extra Sleeping Pods.  
- **Notes**: Check for oxygen recycling system functionality before any journey exceeding 10 days.
