## Stark 1
- **Description**: A compact spaceship designed for solo travelers or small groups, perfect for interplanetary commutes.  
- **Size**: 15 meters in length.  
- **Weight**: 20 metric tons.  
- **Capacity**: 2 passengers.  
- **Color**: Metallic Silver.  
- **Price**: $2,000,000.  
- **Compatible Accessories**: Solar Power Booster, Compact Cargo Pods.  
- **Notes**: Ensure regular maintenance of the ion propulsion system every 500 light-years.
