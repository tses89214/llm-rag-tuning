## Stark 5
- **Description**: Ideal for cargo and trade missions, offering exceptional storage capacity.  
- **Size**: 45 meters in length.  
- **Weight**: 70 metric tons.  
- **Capacity**: 8 passengers and up to 30 metric tons of cargo.  
- **Color**: Industrial Gray.  
- **Price**: $9,500,000.  
- **Compatible Accessories**: Heavy-Duty Storage Racks, Cargo Lifting Cranes.  
- **Notes**: Replace screws in the main cargo hold with **size 32 screws** for stability.
